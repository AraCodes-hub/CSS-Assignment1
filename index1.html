DIFFERENCE BETWEEN PADDING, MARGIN AND BORDER

Padding- The inner space between the content and the border of your box.

Border- The perimeter of the box. Border can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line.

Margin- The outer space(lack of space) surrounding the box.
